<p>All students’ devices are installed with the MOE Device Management Application
(DMA) as part of the measures to enable a safe and conducive learning environment
for students. The purpose of the MOE DMA is to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Keep your child safe online by blocking off undesirable websites</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Allow parents to be aware of their children’s activities on their children’s
devices</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Limit screen time for your child, when necessary.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
